# Mall of America - Data Source

## 1. Fast Facts & Scale
- **Square Footage:** 5.6 million square feet.
- **Visitor Volume:** 40+ million visitors annually (more than Disney World, Grand Canyon, and Graceland combined).
- **Retail Density:** 520+ stores, including flagships like Apple, Zara, and H&M.
- **Walking Distance:** 4.3 miles of storefronts (approx. 7 miles if you walk every level).
- **Economic Impact:** Nearly $2 billion annually for the state of Minnesota.
- **Economic Power:** For every $1 spent at MOA, an additional $1.25 is spent in the surrounding region.

## 2. Demographics & Reach
- **Total Reach:** 30 million people live within a day's drive.
- **Tourist Impact:** 40% of visitors are tourists; they represent 50% of total sales.
- **Household Income (HHI):** Largest concentration of HHI $100k-$250k+ in the MSP market.
- **Luxury Affinity:** 78% of affluent shoppers in the region choose MOA for high-end purchases.
- **International Market:** Significant visitors from Canada, Mexico, Brazil, UK, Germany, Japan, and China.

## 3. Entertainment & Lifestyle (The "Anchor" Attractions)
- **Nickelodeon Universe:** The largest indoor theme park in North America (7 acres of rides).
- **SEA LIFE® Minnesota Aquarium:** 1.3 million-gallon aquarium with an underwater tunnel.
- **Crayola Experience:** 60,000 square feet of interactive family fun.
- **The Fair on 4:** 36,000 square feet of axe-throwing, go-karts, and craft food.
- **B&B Theatres:** 14-screen luxury cinema with heated recliners.
- **Dining:** 60+ restaurants including upscale dining and specialized food halls.

## 4. Events & Brand Platform
- **Annual Events:** 400+ events per year.
- **Exposure:** Positioned as a global platform for celebrity appearances, product launches, and brand activations.
- **Event Capability:** Hosts everything from concerts to corporate conventions and massive community initiatives.

- **Specialty Leasing:** Pop-up shops, kiosks, and temporary inline spaces for fast-moving brands.
